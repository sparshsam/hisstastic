# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 16, 2026

Hiss-Tastic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

Hiss-Tastic is a fully local-first application. It operates entirely on your device with no network communication except for loading the application itself from the web server (or from local storage when installed as a PWA).

### What we DON'T collect:
- No personal information
- No account credentials
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies

### What is stored locally (on your device only):
- **High scores:** Saved to your browser's localStorage or device app storage. Never transmitted.
- **Gameplay stats:** Games played, food eaten, average score. Stored locally. Never transmitted.
- **Theme preference:** Your selected color theme. Stored locally. Never transmitted.
- **Music preference:** Whether background music is enabled. Stored locally. Never transmitted.

None of this data ever leaves your device.

## Network Access

Hiss-Tastic makes no network requests to any server except:
1. Loading the game files from its host (Vercel or local server)
2. Playing background music from the YouTube source link (user-initiated, opt-in)

## Third-Party Services

Hiss-Tastic uses no third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top will be revised. The policy will never be retroactively changed to permit data collection without your explicit consent.
